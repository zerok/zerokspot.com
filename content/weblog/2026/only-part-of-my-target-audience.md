The primary idea for the [blogging calendar](https://zerokspot.com/weblog/2026/01/18/sticking-to-a-blogging-schedule/) I started earlier this year was to be able to find topics that you, the reader, might find interesting. What I overlooked there, though, was that I have the classic writing block: Will someone care what I write about?

Of course I like to write about topics that people other than me might consider useful. The other primary function of this blog is, though, to write about things to force my brain to think them through. These can be things like why I enjoy a certain beer as well as why I think the combination of DuckDB, JSON and Jupyter is amazing. 

So sometimes the target audience of a post is not you (the human sitting in front of some screen somewhere in the world) but my own brain. I hope you still enjoy my rambling 😄

Due to some events during the last couple of weeks I'm currently at a dangerously low energy level. At this point the calendar is on vacation but I'm still trying to write. The calendar will return! There might be a lot of random thoughts along the way 🤣
